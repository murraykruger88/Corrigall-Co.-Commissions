Welcome to Corrigall & Co. Commissions



Corrigall & Co. Commissions is an ongoing project initiated by Cape-Town based art research consultancy Corrigall & Co.

With the guidence of Mary Corrigall, an award-winning art journalist and academic, various writers are commisioned to produce texts, all with an emphasis on the arts.

Corrigall & Co. Commissions is committed to cultivating audiences and contributing towards growing and sustaining arts writing and production on the African continent.
